About Me

I'm a senior studying Business Administration with a concentration in Marketing at the University of Colorado Boulder. Growing up in Colorado, skiing is more than a hobby — it's a way of life. From groomed resort runs to backcountry powder fields, the mountains have shaped who I am.

I've spent two winters as a certified ski instructor at Vail, where I taught guests of all skill levels and developed a deep appreciation for how great coaching transforms people's relationships with the sport. Outside the slopes, I channel that passion into marketing work for outdoor brands.

When I'm not on skis, you'll find me working on data analytics projects, running with my dog, or planning the next expedition.
