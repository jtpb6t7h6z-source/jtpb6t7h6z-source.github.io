How It Started

I've been playing basketball since I was four. Growing up in Bountiful, Utah, it was just always around — pickup games in driveways, rec leagues, and eventually varsity at Woods Cross High School. We made it to the 2022 state championship game, which was a great experience.

These days I play recreationally whenever I can find a court and some free time. It's a good way to compete and stay active, and I've followed the NBA closely my whole life. Kobe Bryant is my favorite player of all time — his work ethic and mentality on the court is something I've always admired.

Nothing beats finding a good court and getting a run in.

My Favorite Teams & Players

Here are my five favorite NBA teams ranked in order, with some of my favorite players from each franchise. Each team is ranked as an ordered list, with standout players in an unordered list beneath.

  1. Utah Jazz — Hard not to root for the home team.
    • Donovan Mitchell — explosive scorer, one of the best guards of his era
    • John Stockton — all-time assists leader, Jazz royalty
    • Karl Malone — the Mailman; one of the most consistent power forwards ever
  2. Los Angeles Lakers — Kobe's team. Enough said.
    • Kobe Bryant — my favorite player ever; the Mamba Mentality is real
    • Shaquille O'Neal — the most dominant center of his era
    • Magic Johnson — maybe the most complete player to ever play the game
  3. Chicago Bulls (all-time) — MJ's dynasty defined the sport.
    • Michael Jordan — the greatest of all time
    • Scottie Pippen — elite on both ends, the perfect co-star
    • Dennis Rodman — the most committed rebounder in NBA history
  4. Golden State Warriors — Greatest shooting team ever assembled.
    • Stephen Curry — changed basketball; the best shooter who's ever lived
    • Klay Thompson — the second-best shooter ever, ice water in his veins
    • Draymond Green — the defensive heart of four championships
  5. BYU Cougars (NCAA) — Easy pick since I'm a student here.
    • Jimmer Fredette — a BYU legend; one of the most fun college players to watch
    • Dallin Hall — reliable floor general, makes the right play every time
    • Trevin Knell — consistent shooter, always shows up when it matters
